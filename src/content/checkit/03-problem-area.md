## Problem Area

### People spend too much time on social media

People are spending more time than ever on social media platforms. While these
apps help with communication, excessive use is linked to issues like social
anxiety, poor sleep, and lower productivity.

The average person spends approximately 2.5 hours daily scrolling through
platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook, with many often prioritising it
over work and personal interactions
