## Second and Third Iterations

### Refining the experience with user feedback

After iterating the design, we conducted further testing to ensure the app
experience was easy to use and intuitive. We used a variety of testing methods
with different types of participants. Some were everyday people, while others
were design students who provided more detailed and usability-focused feedback.

#### Think-Alouds

We asked users to complete tasks while talking through their thoughts. This
gave us useful insight into how they experienced the app and where they faced
any confusion.

#### Heuristic Evaluations

This method helped us gather useful feedback by having people review the app
based on common usability principles. This allowed us to quickly highlight
problems that might make the app confusing, allowing us to improve the overall
user experience.

#### SUS Forms

We used the System Usability Scale (SUS) to collect quick, quantitative
feedback. Users rated their experience through the likert scale, giving us
clear scores to measure the app’s usability.
