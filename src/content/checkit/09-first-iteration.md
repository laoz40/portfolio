## First Iteration

### Prototyping and testing the experience

Using Figma, we made the first wireframes of what our app interface would begin
to look like. I created the initial wireframe prototype by including additional
screens to create a more holistic experience for usability testing.

image

With the main features finalised, we moved on to usability testing to guide the
next stage of iteration. We created a test plan and cognitive walkthrough
script to observe how users interacted with the app. Our goal was to see if it
was easy to use and fit into users' daily lives. By watching users and
collecting feedback, we gained valuable insights to refine the app based on
real-world use.

image

The initial iteration received positive feedback from testing. Participants
felt the app would be helpful in boosting productivity and could fit it into
their daily lives. They felt it would help them use social media less and stay
focused on tasks.
