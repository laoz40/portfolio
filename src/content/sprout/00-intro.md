# Sprout

I led a team of four to design a mobile AR app that helps encourage people to
plant trees. We wanted people to make simple steps in helping the
environment. We ran user research to learn what motivates sustainable
behaviour, then built and tested prototypes to see how people used the app.
Through many rounds of user testing, we refined the app to make it easy and clear
to use.
