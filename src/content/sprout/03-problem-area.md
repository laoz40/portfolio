## Problem Area

### Deforestation is ruining our planet

Deforestation results in the destruction of natural habitats, which leads to the extinction of many plant and animal species causing a decline in biodiversity worldwide. Since 2000, 488 million hectares of tree cover have been lost, leading to serious environmental damage.
