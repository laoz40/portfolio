carouselItems:
  - title: AR Experience
    description: Users enjoyed the AR experience and the concept of visualising future tree growth. Many stated this would motivate them to plant and care for their trees. However, some users found the AR experience wasn't intuitive, as plant information was fixed and didn't rotate with them, requiring movement to a specific spot to read it.
  - title: Community Engagement
    description: The leaderboard feature was well received, and users stated it would provide additional motivation as they try to beat their friends.
  - title: Select Plant Preference
    description: A/B testing revealed that users preferred the dropdown menu compared to the search bar when selecting a plant. It felt more intuitive as users could see all inputs that were available to them.
  - title: More Information Needed
    description: Participants wanted to see more detailed information throughout the app, especially whilst choosing between different plant species. They wanted information such as the level of difficulty in growing each species, which ones are ideal for beginners, and which are low-maintenance options.
  - title: Design Consistency
    description: Some users struggled with navigation, due to deviations from typical app conventions, making it hard to access screens like the profile and environmental impact stats.
