I first started "coding" at 11 by editing Team Fortress 2 (video game) HUDs. I loved
tweaking the colours and animations, editing files that were similar to CSS. I've
always enjoyed editing aesthetics and making the stuff I use look nice (ricing).
It's why I love using Android and Linux.

Years later, this personality trait is probably what made me choose to study UX
design an uni. I came to enjoy learning how to design and build sites using
HTML and CSS.
