I studied UX design at The University of Sydney (Bachelor of Design Computing).
I had so much fun learning about user research, prototyping, and usability
testing. Designing new and innovative products was easily my favourite part of
the course.

It also changed the way I approach building things. I became much more aware of
design principles and user psychology, and started considering things I would
not have thought about before.

User testing especially made a big impression on me. It showed me that
you can never be completely sure about a design. Other people will always
notice things you would never have considered, which is why it's so important.
